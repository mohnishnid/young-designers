Digital Vaccination Tracker for Children Aged 0 through 18 Years
The project aimed to design a vaccination tracker with a reminder feature for Mfine users and the doctor app. The first phase of the project was to handle the data entry for digital vaccination for doctors. Primary and secondary research was carried out to understand the context and needs of the doctors better. The solutions were iterated to improve the autofill feature and information representation of the interface. After designing the feature for the doctors, the data entry interface was designed for users. The iterations varied based on ease of use and user delight. Lastly, a vaccination reminder flow was designed for parents to notify them about due dates. After several iterations, the designs were approved by the product manager. As a final handoff, the design mocks were consolidated and sent to the team for application development.
Keywords: child immunization, digital health records, healthcare, vaccination reminder, vaccination tracker
